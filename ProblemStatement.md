# **AI Interviewer System**

## **1. Problem Statement**
Recruitment processes often require multiple interview rounds and significant time from hiring managers and HR teams. Conducting interviews manually for a large number of candidates can be time-consuming and may delay the hiring process.

The goal of this project is to develop an AI-powered interviewer that can automatically conduct candidate interviews, ask relevant questions, analyze responses, and evaluate candidate skills.

## **2. Requirement**
The AI Interviewer is an intelligent system that conducts automated interviews with candidates. The system asks job-related questions, records and analyzes candidate responses, and generates an evaluation report.

It can support both text-based and voice-based interviews and helps organizations screen candidates more efficiently before human interview rounds.

## **3. Key Features**
- Automated interview question generation  
- Text or voice-based candidate interaction  
- AI analysis of candidate responses  
- Skill and knowledge evaluation  
- Automated interview reports  
- Helps HR teams screen candidates faster  

## **4. How the System Works**
1. Recruiter defines the job role and required skills.  
2. The system generates interview questions based on the role.  
3. Candidates answer questions through text or voice.  
4. The AI analyzes responses for correctness, clarity, and relevance.  
5. A candidate evaluation report is generated.
